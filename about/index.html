About The Ozark Gazette

Your Source for Ozark News, Weather, Sports and Traffic

The Ozark Gazette is a Tecumseh, Missouri-based community news publication serving Ozark County and the surrounding Missouri Ozarks.

Who We Are

The Ozark Gazette is operated by Cook Global News Network and was founded in 2026 to provide reliable local coverage for Tecumseh, Ozark County and the surrounding Ozarks communities.

Our mission is to deliver practical, community-centered reporting for readers who want clear information about local news, weather, traffic, public affairs, sports, markets, obituaries, court records, schools, community life and events across the Ozarks.

What We Cover

The Ozark Gazette covers Local, US, World, Politics, Investigations, Markets, Technology, Opinion, Environment, Entertainment, Obituaries, Weather, Sports and Traffic. Weather Radar is part of our Weather section, and our Market Center is powered by CGN-style market coverage.

We also provide daily reader features including Horoscopes, Sudoku, Crosswords and Puzzles.

Local First

Our coverage begins in Tecumseh and Ozark County, including Gainesville, Theodosia, Bakersfield, Dora, Thornfield, Isabella, Pontiac, Wasola, Udall and nearby rural communities. We pay special attention to US Highway 160, Ozark County roads, severe weather, local schools, county government, public safety, court records, community announcements and obituaries.

Powered by CGN

The Ozark Gazette uses Cook Global News Network infrastructure for article publishing, account access, subscription tools, weather, market data, traffic features and digital news presentation. The result is a local newspaper experience with modern CGN technology behind it.
