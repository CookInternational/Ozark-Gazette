Michael A. Cook

Editor-in-Chief

Beats: Editorial leadership, Politics, World, Markets, public accountability, investigations and major public-affairs coverage.

A Chicagoland native, Michael A. Cook is the Editor-in-Chief of CGN News. A veteran of the Army National Guard and a Ball State University alumnus, Michael brings a grounded, real-world perspective to coverage of Politics, World, and Markets. His editorial focus centers on public accountability, institutional decision-making, economic change, and the national and global developments that shape everyday life. At CGN News, he leads with an emphasis on clear reporting, independent analysis, and coverage that connects major events to their real-world impact.

editor@cgnnews.net

The Ozark Gazette Newsroom

Newsroom Reporter

Beats: Weather briefs, traffic updates, sports roundups, community notices, public records and source-led daily service coverage.

The Newsroom Reporter represents the desk work behind many daily service updates. The newsroom reviews official weather alerts, road information, sports schedules, public records and community notices, then turns that source material into clear reader-friendly briefs for Ozark County. Sensitive items, including deaths, courts, crime and investigations, remain subject to editorial review before publication.

tips@cgnnews.net

Jordan Whitaker

Staff Reporter

Beats: Local news, county government, public safety, roads, schools and community life.

Jordan Whitaker covers local stories across Tecumseh, Gainesville, Theodosia, Bakersfield, Dora, Thornfield, Isabella, Pontiac, Wasola, Udall and the Missouri Ozarks. The Staff Reporter role focuses on the practical decisions, road issues, meetings, school events and public-service information that shape everyday life in Ozark County.

tips@cgnnews.net

Ozark Weather Desk

Weather Desk

Beats: Tecumseh weather, radar, National Weather Service alerts, flooding, heat, winter weather and travel impacts.

The Weather Desk translates official forecasts, watches, warnings and radar information into practical guidance for Ozark County readers, especially when storms, heat, winter weather or flooding could affect roads, schools, farms and community events.

tips@cgnnews.net

Ozark Traffic Desk

Traffic Desk

Beats: US-160, MO-5, MoDOT traveler updates, crash sources, flooding, low-water crossings and Ozark County roads.

The Traffic Desk follows official transportation and public-safety sources for road conditions affecting Tecumseh and the wider Missouri Ozarks. Coverage focuses on practical travel information, verified closures, weather-related road impacts and source-linked updates for readers before they head out.

tips@cgnnews.net

Ozark Sports Desk

Sports Desk

Beats: Gainesville, Bakersfield, Dora, Lutie, Thornfield, youth sports and regional college sports.

The Sports Desk follows school athletics, schedules, scores and community sports moments that matter to families across Ozark County and the wider Missouri Ozarks.

tips@cgnnews.net

Obituaries & Records Desk

Community Records

Beats: Obituaries, community records, public notices, court records and local milestones.

The Community Records Desk handles source-led records items with care, accuracy and respect. Obituary and court-record items are reviewed for attribution, duplication, privacy and legal sensitivity before publication.

tips@cgnnews.net
