How to Create an Account

Create or sign in to an account before subscribing so your access attaches to the correct email.

Open the account page

Go to the Account or Login area from the site header, footer, support links, or article paywall.

Create your account

Choose the account creation option and enter the requested email and login details.

Use the same email

Use the same email address when subscribing, contacting support, or checking subscription access.

Sign in before subscribing

If you already have an account, sign in first, then continue through the Account page.

How to Subscribe

  1. Sign in or create an account first.
  2. Open the Account page.
  3. Choose the subscription option inside Account.
  4. Continue to PayPal checkout.
  5. Complete the PayPal payment.
  6. Return to The Ozark Gazette after payment if prompted.

How PayPal Checkout Works

The Ozark Gazette may send readers to a PayPal payment flow for subscription checkout. Complete the payment through PayPal, then return to The Ozark Gazette if prompted.

  • Use the same account email when possible.
  • Save your PayPal transaction reference if payment-related help is needed.
  • Contact support if access does not update after payment.

Article Access and Paywall Help

If an article does not open as expected, the most common causes are account status, a different email, browser cache, private browsing mode, or an interrupted payment/session.

  • Sign out and sign back in.
  • Confirm you are using the right account email.
  • Refresh the article page.
  • Try another browser or device.
  • Email support if the issue continues.

Newsletter and Email Help

Use the same email for newsletters and account access when possible. If emails do not arrive, check spam, junk, promotions, and blocked-sender settings.

  • Newsletter signup and unsubscribe help.
  • Missing email or confirmation questions.
  • Account and newsletter email mismatch support.

Troubleshooting Checklist

Try these quick steps before contacting support.

Refresh and sign in again

Refresh the page, then sign out and sign back in if account access looks wrong.

Check your email identity

Confirm your account email matches the email used for subscription checkout or support.

Try another browser

If the page still appears old or broken, try another browser or device and clear cached data if needed.

Confirm payment completed

For subscription issues, check that PayPal shows the payment or subscription as completed.

Copy the page URL

Send the exact URL where the issue happened so support can review the right page.

Include screenshots

Screenshots can help show login, access, payment, or page-display problems clearly.
